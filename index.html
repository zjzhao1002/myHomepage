About Me:

I am a particle physcisist. My researches include many aspects: Monte-Carlo event generators, effective field theory, collider physics, and physics beyond the standard models. More details of my researches can be found in my publications.

Most of my works are theoretical studies and numerical simulations, so I develops my skills on programming. The most useful languages are C++, Fortran, and Python.

I am also interested in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. These technologies have been widely used in researches of particle physics. I want to master them.
